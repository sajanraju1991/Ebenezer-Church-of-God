EVANGELISM
"And he said unto them, Go ye into all the world, and preach the gospel to the whole creation." Mark 16:15

Evangelism

An important mandate that Jesus gave His disciples 2000 years ago was to 'go and make disciples of all nations'. Even today, believers who have experienced the indescribable love of Jesus and have received the gift of salvation through Him, strive to give what they have freely received. This love of God compels us to partner in the cause of advancing the Gospel in every possible way.

As a believer, it is a responsibility given by God to proclaim the gospel of Jesus Christ. For God so loved the world that he gave his one and only Son, that whoever believes in him shall not perish but have eternal life. Our evangelism consist of distribution of tracts, preaching on the street, door to door evangelism and singing songs of praise, placards.

" As you go, proclaim this message: ‘The kingdom of heaven has come near.’ Heal the sick, raise the dead, cleanse those who have leprosy,[a] drive out demons. Freely you have received; freely give."
Mathew 10:7, 8

Ebenezer Church of God

Ebenezer Church of God is a Pentecostal community based in Addlestone, Surrey, and Slough, Berkshire, dedicated to demonstrating the unconditional and overwhelming love of God through the power of the Holy Spirit. As a church, we strive to guide people in understanding and living by the Word of God, teaching everyone in all wisdom that we may present every man perfect and complete in all the will of God. In a world where sin has a hold on many individuals, our church is a place where you can be set free from the bondage of sin through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ and find peace that surpasses all understanding.

Our Mission


Our mission is to spread the message of salvation through the death and resurrection of Jesus Christ and to strengthen believers through baptism in the name of the Father, the Son and the Holy Spirit. Salvation gives the promise of eternal life to all those who accept Him. We aim to fulfil the Great Communion of Jesus Christ to make disciples of all nations, teaching people to observe things that God has commanded. Through the spirit filled worship and coming together to praise the Lord, we believe in fostering a sense of community within the church between all the believers to encourage and strengthen one another. As we are dedicated to spread the love of God to people all around us, helping the needy and addressing social issues are fundamental aspects of our ministry.

 

Our Administration

This fellowship is strengthened with a devoted and efficient administration which includes a committed pastor’s council, an outstanding choir team, an active Youth committee, a dynamic ladies’ ministry and a flourishing Sunday School department. By God’s steadfast love and grace, every single department have been working tirelessly to advance the duty and purpose of the church, assuring its seamless and fruitful functioning.

We Belive

  • The Holy Trinity : We believe in One God eternally existing in three forms – God the Father, God the Son and God the Holy Spirit

  • Salvation: Salvation is by faith in Jesus Christ, through repentance, forgiveness of sins and a personal relationship with God. Salvation is considered a gift to mankind instead of something earned by good deeds.

  • Baptism of the Holy Spirit: All those who repent their sins should be baptized in the name of the Father, Son and the Holy Spirit.

  • Speaking in tongues: It is considered a gift of the Holy Spirit which allows believers to communicate with God in spiritual language.

  • Power of Prayer: Prayer is used to communicate and establish a personal relationship with God. Prayer is the key to seek guidance and finding strength in times of need.

  • Second coming: The second coming is the returning of Jesus Christ mid-air, where the righteous dead will be resurrected the righteous living will be transformed and taken up.

  • Evangelism: We are committed to spreading the gospel of Jesus Christ and making disciples of all nations.

  • Living a holy/blameless life: As it is written in the Word, “Be holy, for I am holy.” (1 Peter 1:16)
